How it works

  1. Set your focus, Pomodoro, or time-block session inside the desktop app.
  2. Launch the floating overlay to keep the countdown within view.
  3. Pause, stop, or embrace a break right from the overlay controls.
  4. Review your progress in the built-in study log and celebrate with Snorlax.
